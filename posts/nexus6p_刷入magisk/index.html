Nexus6p_刷入magisk

TWRP msgisk magisk manager magisckSU magisk Hide resetprop

Magisk 主页 v18.0 https://forum.xda-developers.com/apps/magisk https://github.com/topjohnwu/Magisk/releases

TWRP Nexus 6p https://dl.twrp.me/angler/ https://twrp.me/Devices/

手动进入bootloaer adb reboot bootloaer

开启oem解锁 fastboot oem unlock //fastboot flashing unlock

开始root操作 [Magisk-v18.0.zip 拷贝到手机下载目录] fastboot flash recovery twrp-3.2.3-0-angler.img

此时，用音量将界面选择到 [recovery mode]; 然后按电源键;

进入 twrp，点击 [install], 选择拷贝到下载目录的 [Magisk-v18.0.zip]
