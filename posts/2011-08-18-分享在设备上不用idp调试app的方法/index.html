<p style="text-align: left; padding: 0px; margin: 0.8em 0px 0.8em 0px;">
  操作步骤：
</p>

<p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
  <span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">iPhone</span>必须是越狱后的，并且安装了<span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">afc2add</span>。
</p>

<p class="p2" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; min-height: 18px; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
  <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
    将<span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">iPhone</span>接入<span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">Mac</span>，并打开<span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">xCode</span>，会自动弹出<span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">Organizer</span>（或在<span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">xCode</span>的菜单<span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">Window</span>中打开），在其中点击<span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">“</span>用这台设备进行开发<span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">”</span>
  </p>
  
  <p class="p2" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; min-height: 18px; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
    <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
      xCode<span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">配置步骤：</span>
    </p>
    
    <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
      零、开始前的注意事项
    </p>
    
    <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
      <span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">1</span>、<span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;"> </span>权限问题：
    </p>
    
    <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
      <span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">   </span>要想修改并保存文件，须先修改该文件及其上层目录的权限（在右键菜单的显示简介中），添加当前用户并赋读和写权限，才能修改文件。
    </p>
    
    <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
      <span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">2</span>、注意备份
    </p>
    
    <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
      <span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">   </span>修改之前一定注意备份原有文件
    </p>
    
    <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
      <span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">一、修改文件</span>SDKSettings.plist
    </p>
    
    <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
      <span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;"> </span>文件位置：
    </p>
    
    <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
         /Developer/Platforms/iPhoneOS.platform/Developer/SDKs/iPhoneOS4.3.sdk
    </p>
    
    <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
      <span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">  </span>修改方法：
    </p>
    
    <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
          <span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">找到</span><key>CODE_SIGNING_REQUIRED</key><string>YES</string>
    </p>
    
    <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
      <span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">    </span>将值从YES改为<span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">NO</span>
    </p>
    
    <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
          <span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">找到</span><key>ENTITLEMENTS_REQUIRED</key><string>YES</string>
    </p>
    
    <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
      <span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">    </span>将值从YES改为<span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">NO</span>
    </p>
    
    <p class="p2" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; min-height: 18px; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
      <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
        <span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">二、修改文件</span>info.plist
      </p>
      
      <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
        <span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;"> </span>文件位置：
      </p>
      
      <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
          /Developer/Platforms/iPhoneOS.platform/
      </p>
      
      <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
        <span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">  </span>修改方法：
      </p>
      
      <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
          <span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">找到所有的</span><key>CODE_SIGN_CONTEXT_CLASS</key><string>XCiPhoneOSCodeSignContext</string>
      </p>
      
      <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
          <span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">将值从</span>XCiPhoneOSCodeSignContext<span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">改为</span> XCCodeSignContext
      </p>
      
      <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
        （共<span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">2</span>处）
      </p>
      
      <p class="p2" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; min-height: 18px; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
        <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
          三、打开终端，输入以下命令：
        </p>
        
        <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
          cd /Developer/Platforms/iPhoneOS.platform/Developer/Library/Xcode/Plug-ins/iPhoneOS Build System Support.xcplugin/Contents/MacOS/
        </p>
        
        <p class="p2" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; min-height: 18px; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
          <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
            dd if=iPhoneOS Build System Support of=working bs=500 count=255
          </p>
          
          <p class="p2" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; min-height: 18px; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
            <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
              printf &#8220;xc3x26x00x00&#8221; >> working
            </p>
            
            <p class="p2" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; min-height: 18px; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
              <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
                dd if=iPhoneOS Build System Support of=working bs=1 skip=127504 seek=127504
              </p>
              
              <p class="p2" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; min-height: 18px; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
                <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
                  /bin/mv -n iPhoneOS Build System Support iPhoneOS Build System Support.original
                </p>
                
                <p class="p2" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; min-height: 18px; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
                  <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
                    /bin/mv working iPhoneOS Build System Support
                  </p>
                  
                  <p class="p2" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; min-height: 18px; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
                    <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
                      chmod a+x iPhoneOS Build System Support
                    </p>
                    
                    <p class="p2" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; min-height: 18px; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
                      <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
                        <span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">四、修改工程属性（</span>Project Settings<span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">）</span>
                      </p>
                      
                      <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
                        先打开工程属性，<span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">xCode3</span>可在菜单<span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">File->Project<br /> Settings</span>中打开，或右键点工程文件列表中的工程项，从中找到工程属性；<span class="s1" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">xCode4</span>直接单击工程文件列表中的工程项即可
                      </p>
                      
                      <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
                        <span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">在工程属性中，找到</span>Build Settings<span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">，修改其中的</span>Code<br /> Signing Identity<span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">，将所有属性值都从</span>iPhone Developer<span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">改为</span>Don&#8217;t<br /> Code Sign
                      </p>
                      
                      <p class="p3" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal Times; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
                        <span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">（若在</span>xCode4<span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">中找不到</span>Code<br /> Signing Identity<span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">，在属性列表的左上方有个</span>“Basic”<span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">和</span>“All”<span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">的选项，选择</span>All<span class="s2" style="font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">就能找到了。</span>
                      </p>
                      
                      <p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: left; font: 15px STSong; min-height: 15px;">
                        <p class="p4" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: left; font: 15px STSong; min-height: 15px;">
                          <p class="p4" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; min-height: 15px; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
                            <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
                              最后、选择Device的SDK，Run。会将编译好的app传输到设备上，不过xCode会提示Error Starting Executable &#8220;project name&#8221;, Error launching remote program…这时，只需手动在设备上打开这个程序就可以了，调试信息可在Organizer里实时监控
                            </p>
                            
                            <p class="p1" style="text-align: left; font: normal normal normal 15px/normal STSong; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">

                                再次最后，操作完quit一下Xcode，再打开往设备上安装app。
